# Waymakers Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 2, 2026**

## Overview

Waymakers ("we", "our", "the app") is a small group organization app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Display name
- Account creation date

**Content You Create:**
- Group information (name, description)
- Chat messages within groups
- Calendar events
- Curriculum/lesson content
- Bible verse annotations and notes

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Provide and operate the app
- Allow you to communicate with your group members
- Display your name and contributions to other group members
- Send notifications about group activity

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your activity outside the app
- Share your data with anyone outside your groups

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Storage). Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

## Data Sharing

Your content (messages, annotations, curriculum notes) is shared only with members of the groups you belong to. No data is shared with third parties except as required by law.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- You can leave a group at any time
- Group leaders can delete their groups, which removes all associated content
- To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below

## Children's Privacy

Waymakers is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## Common Questions

**How do I create a group?**
Tap the + button on the My Groups screen and select "Create Group."

**How do I join a group?**
Tap the + button and select "Join Group." Enter the 6-character invite code from your group leader.

**How do I leave a group?**
Go to the group's Info tab and tap "Leave Group."

**How do I add a Bible annotation?**
Open the Bible tab in your group, navigate to a chapter, and tap any verse to add a note.

**How do I delete my account?**
Email us at jessemlinford@gmail.com and we'll remove your account and all associated data.
